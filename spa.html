Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Advanced Sample Document Assistant (ASDA)

Can I get custom documents or modifications to existing templates?

Yes, we can accommodate custom requests! While ASDA provides a comprehensive set of industry-specific document types, we understand that every business has unique needs.

To request custom documents:
Submit a feedback request with details about your specific requirements. Our team will review your request and work with you to create custom templates or modify existing ones to meet your business needs.

What to include in your custom request:

  • Type of document you need
  • Industry and sub-industry specifics
  • Special clauses or terms required
  • Jurisdiction requirements
  • Timeline for completion
What industries and document types are supported?

ASDA supports multiple industries with specialized document types for each:

Industries:

  • Healthcare - Medical Device, Digital Health, Clinical Research, etc.
  • Financial Services - Banking, Investment, Insurance, Fintech, etc.
  • Technology - Software, Hardware, AI/ML, Cybersecurity, etc.
  • Energy - Oil & Gas, Wind, Solar, Nuclear, etc.
  • Manufacturing - Automotive, Aerospace, Electronics, etc.
  • Real Estate - Commercial, Residential, Development, etc.
  • HR - Employment, Benefits, Training, etc.

Each industry includes dozens of specialized document types with appropriate legal obligations and clauses. Visit the Document Types to explore all available options or the User Guide to learn how to use ASDA effectively.

How accurate are the generated documents?

ASDA generates professional-quality legal documents with industry-specific terminology, realistic business terms, and proper legal formatting. These are sample documents created exclusively for demonstration purposes.

Document characteristics:

  • All financial terms, dates, and party information are generated examples
  • Documents are intended for educational, training, and demonstration purposes
  • Generated content showcases document structure and legal terminology
  • Examples demonstrate realistic contract language and formatting

The AI system uses extensive legal databases and industry best practices to ensure documents follow proper structure and include relevant obligations for each document type.

How do I access my generated documents?

Once your documents are generated, you'll receive notifications via both email and Slack with direct links to access your documents.

What you'll receive:

  • Email notification with a direct link to your Google Drive folder
  • User Guide with document types filtered for your industry/sub-industry
  • Slack notification with document access links

All documents are stored in Google Drive as both PDF and Google Docs formats for easy access and editing.

Can I generate documents in sets or individually?

Yes! ASDA offers two generation modes:

Document Sets (5+ documents):

  • Creates complete contract ecosystems (NDA â†’ MSA â†’ SOW â†’ Change Orders)
  • Documents reference each other with proper parent-child relationships
  • Consistent parties, terms, and dates across all documents in the set
  • Minimum quantity: 5 documents per request

Individual Documents (any quantity):

  • Generates standalone documents of various types
  • Each document is independent with its own parties and terms
  • Suitable for diverse document libraries and training materials
  • Any quantity from 1 to 50+ documents
How long does document generation take?

Document generation time varies based on quantity and complexity:

  • 1-5 documents: 2-5 minutes
  • 6-15 documents: 5-10 minutes
  • 16+ documents: 10-20 minutes

Factors affecting generation time include:

  • Document complexity and length
  • Language translation requirements
  • Industry-specific legal obligations
  • Current system load

You'll receive notifications as soon as your documents are ready, so you don't need to wait on the page.

Is there a limit to how many documents I can generate?

ASDA allows flexible document generation with reasonable limits:

  • Per request: Up to 50 documents
  • Daily limits: Contact us for high-volume needs
  • Document sets: Minimum 5 documents (generates in multiples of 5)

For enterprise or high-volume usage requirements, please submit a feedback request with your specific needs and we'll work with you to accommodate them.
